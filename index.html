Polling Booth Guide

Follow these guidelines to ensure a smooth and legal voting process.

The DO's

Maintain Decorum

Stand in the queue and wait for your turn patiently. Silence is mandatory.

Get Your Finger Inked

The indelible ink on your left forefinger is the proud mark of your vote.

Check Voter List

Verify your name and serial number on the electoral roll outside the booth.

Carry Valid ID

Bring your Voter ID (EPIC) or other ECI-approved documents like Aadhar.

The DON'Ts

No Electronics

Mobile phones, smartwatches, and gadgets are strictly forbidden inside.

No Political Symbols

Do not wear any clothing or caps with party logos or candidate names.

No Photography

Taking photos of the EVM, VVPAT, or the voting process is a legal offense.

No Double Voting

Attempting to vote more than once is a serious crime under electoral law.
